codeIndore( ) is a student technical community for the students in Indore

This Hacktoberfest themed hackathon is for us to celebrate the spirit of open-source software together.

Come and join over 150 like-minded students contributing to open-source during a weekend to make something amazing!

Please keep in mind our code of conduct.

F.A.Q.


Can I participate?


You must be at least 18 years old and a student,
or have graduated from university in the past twelve months.


How much does it cost?


Thanks to our sponsors, attendance is free.
We will provide you with meals, swag, Wi-Fi and a workspace.


What should I bring?


Your laptop, phone, and chargers. You may bring a small pillow or blanket. Please check with us before bringing anything else
Please bring a valid ID for admission.



Where is it?


The details of the Venue will be updated soon



Where will I sleep?


We have a sleeping area which will be separate from the working area.



Will my travel be reimbursed?


Travel reimbursements will not be provided. If you cannot make it to us, we encourage you to contribute to open-source from your location.



What about intellectual property?


We do not claim any rights on any of your intellectual property whatsoever.
Everything you create, at our hackathon or not, belongs to you. We do however encourage that you release the source code under an open-source license
